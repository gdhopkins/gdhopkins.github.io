Final Project

This project, produced by myself and Tyreese, is a dots and boxes game.
For those unfamiliar, Dots and Boxes is a game where players take turns
drawing lines between dots in a grid. Players score points by drawing the
final side of any box, and the winner is the player with the most points.

In this Javascript receration of the game, there are options for a
resizable board and a reset button to easily play multiple rounds
with the same dimensions. It's a simple point and click program,
no keyboard neccessary!
